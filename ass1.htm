Aves Bird Sanctuary

Abode to many of the world's rarest birds ! 


        The Aves bird Sanctuary is roughly 4 square Kilometres in size and
        is situated at the entrance of Noida in Gautam Budh Nagar district
        of Uttar Pradesh.It is situated at a point where river Yamuna enters
        in the state of Uttar Pradesh leaving the territory of Delhi.
        Notified this as Sanctuary in the year 1990.It is now one of the
        466 IBAs (Important Bird Areas) in India
      


    More than 320 bird species recorded out of these  
    most famous are 20 common resident water bird species 
    and 44 common resident woodland species and 43
    migratory water birds!
